Recent Highlights

I recently graduated with my Ph.D. and joined CIERA at Northwestern as a Postdoctoral Fellow.

My JWST proposal (GO 6811, PI: L. Kwok) to observe the extremely faint SN Iax 2024vjm was approved!

My JWST Cycle 3+4 proposal (GO 5232, PI: Kwok) was approved. We will use JWST to collect early-time MIR spectroscopy of normal SN Ia and peculiar subclasses.
